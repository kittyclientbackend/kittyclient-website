01 / About

Built for builders. Loved by players.

KittyClient is a Minecraft utility mod and client platform created by two developers who wanted something more — a client that takes building seriously, adds genuine fun, and carries its own visual identity. It is a real product, built with real craft, and kept alive entirely by its community.

Utility Mod

80+ modules covering building, automation, games, and visual tools — all under one unified client.

Community Funded

Two Founders

One builder. One coder. Both dedicated to making Minecraft more interesting for enthusiasts.

02 The Client What we built

More than a toggle list.

KittyClient was built with a clear vision: a Minecraft utility client that respects builders, integrates games and interactive experiences, and feels like a real, cohesive product. Every module, visual layer, and system belongs to the same world — one where practical utility and personality live together.

From gradient block placers and shape visualisers to chess, snake, a music DAW, and a full 3D UI — KittyClient covers genuine creative work and real fun inside a single identity. It is a platform for builders and Minecraft enthusiasts who want more out of their client.

04 How It Works The donation model

Supported by the community.

KittyClient runs on a monthly donation model. There are no forced purchases and no locked-away core features — just a community that chooses to support the work. Every donor directly funds continued development, new modules, and platform improvements. We are deeply grateful for every person who backs this project.

Monthly Support

Access is tied to an active monthly donation. As long as the community supports it, KittyClient keeps updating.

Genuinely Appreciated
