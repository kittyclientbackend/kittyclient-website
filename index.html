01 Manifesto

Not a toggle pile. A full client world.

KittyClient is built around a simple idea: Minecraft should feel powerful without feeling cold. Utility and personality are treated as part of the same system.

One side of KittyClient is utility: chat tools, navigation systems, HUD upgrades, building support, camera tools, automation, storage, privacy features, safety features, and general gameplay convenience that make the client feel capable and sharp.

The other side is personality: custom themes, animated menus, 3D interface ideas, sound, motion, social presence, visual experiments, and playful modules that give the client character. Together they make KittyClient feel like a platform, not a small addon.
