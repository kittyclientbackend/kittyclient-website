03 FAQ

Support FAQ.

I created an account but still cannot sign in.

If the account was created through Google, finish the client password step on the account page so the same identity can also sign in through the launcher and client.

What kind of client is KittyClient?

It is a futuristic Minecraft client platform that combines immersive UI, powerful modules, creative tools, social systems, privacy-minded design, and a strong visual identity into one experience.

What makes KittyClient different?

It balances practical tools with personality. Serious utility, visual experimentation, social presence, atmosphere, and playful systems all belong to the same world.

What is Purritone?

Purritone is KittyClient's automation system. It handles movement and tasks on your behalf — pathfinding, automated building, resource gathering, and goal-driven actions. Set an objective and let it run.
