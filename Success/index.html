Get set up in four steps.

The loader JAR is the only file you need. Your subscription is verified automatically every time you log in — no keys, no codes.

01
Download the loader

Click the download button. Save the JAR file anywhere — you will move it in step two.

02
Add to your mods folder

Drop the JAR into your Minecraft mods folder. For Prism Launcher: right-click the instance → Folder → mods.

03
Launch Minecraft with Fabric 1.21.4

KittyClient requires Fabric for Minecraft 1.21.4. The Fabric API mod must also be present in the mods folder.

04
Sign in with your account email

The KittyClient login screen appears at launch. Use the same email and password as this account. Your subscription is verified server-side automatically.
